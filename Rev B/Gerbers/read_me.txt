Breaking Bitcoin PCB, Rev B
06/28/2017

Files included:
	gerbers.zip		A zip file of all PCB gerbers

PCB Fab Requirements:
	- 2-Layer PCB Stackup
	- 1oz Top and Bottom Copper Thickness
	- Finish: Lead Free HASL
	- Soldermask on top and bottom
	- Silkscreen on top and bottom
	- PCB Thickness: 0.062"
	- PCB Material: FR-04

Gerber File Naming Conventions
	breaking_bicoin.toplayer.ger		Top Copper
	breaking_bicoin.tpaste.ger		Top Paste
	breaking_bicoin.topsilkscreeen.ger	Top Silkscreen
	breaking_bicoin.topsoldermask.ger	Top Soldermask
	breaking_bicoin.bottomlayer.ger		Bottom Copper
	breaking_bicoin.bpaste.ger		Bottom Paste
	breaking_bicoin.bottomsilkscreeen.ger	Bottom Silkscreen
	breaking_bicoin.bottomsoldermask.ger	Bottom Soldermask
	breaking_bicoin.boardoutline.ger	PCB Outline
	breaking_bicoin.drills.xln		Drill File, Excellon 24 format
